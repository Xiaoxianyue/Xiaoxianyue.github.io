01

给定句子： John reads a book, 基于2元文法3元文法

二元文法：

(<BOS>, John)  
(John, reads)  
(reads, a)  
(a, book)  
(book, <EOS>)

三元文法：

(<BOS>, John, reads)  
(John, reads, a)  
(reads, a, book)  
(a, book, <EOS>)

02

给定句子：<BOS> John reads a book <EOS>, 基于2元文法求概率

P(<BOS> John reads a book <EOS>)=P(John∣<BOS>)⋅P(reads∣John)⋅P(a∣reads)⋅P(book∣a)⋅P(<EOS>∣book)

进一步展开就是：

P(<BOS> John reads a book <EOS>)=P(<BOS>,John)P(<BOS>)⋅P(John,reads)P(John)⋅P(reads,a)P(reads)⋅P(a,book)P(a)⋅P(book,<EOS>)P(book)

03

给定汉语：他是研究生物的，采用二元文法写概率

<BOS> 他 是 研究 生物 的 <EOS>
P(seg)=P(他∣<BOS>)⋅P(是∣他)⋅P(研究∣是)⋅P(生物∣研究)⋅P(的∣生物)⋅P(<EOS>∣的)P(Cstring)=P(他∣<BOS>)⋅P(是∣他)⋅P(研∣是)⋅P(究∣研)⋅P(生∣究)⋅P(物∣生)⋅P(的∣物)⋅P(<EOS>∣的)

给定语料库：

{<BOS>John reads Moby Dick EOS}

{<BOS>Mary reads a different book<EOS>}

{<BOS>She reads a book by Cher <EOS>}

求 John reads a book 的概率

P(<BOS> John reads a book <EOS>)=P(John∣<BOS>)⋅P(reads∣John)⋅P(a∣reads)⋅P(book∣a)⋅P(<EOS>∣book)P(John reads a book)=13⋅11⋅23⋅12⋅12

06

Cher reads a book

P(Cher reads a book)=03⋅01⋅23⋅12⋅12

加一平滑：

  1. P(Cher∣<BOS>)：
=0+13+13=116
  1. P(reads∣Cher)：
=0+11+13=114
  1. P(a∣reads)：
=2+13+13=316
  1. P(book∣a)：
=1+12+13=215
  1. P(<EOS>∣book)：
=1+12+13=215P(Cher reads a book)=116⋅114⋅316⋅215⋅215
